Website Planning Document

Site Name:

Healthy Quick Recipes

This name represents a website dedicated to providing healthy meals that can be prepared in 30 minutes or less. The goal is to help busy individuals eat nutritious food without spending hours in the kitchen

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote healthy eating by offering a collection simple, fast, and nutritious Recipes. Visitors can submit their own quick recipe and subscribe to receive weekly recipe ideas via emali.

Scenerios

  • What are some quick vegetarian recipe I can make for dinner tonight?
  • How can I prepare healthy lunches in under an hour for the week?

Color Schema

Sea Green (#2e8b57):Used for headings, buttons and accents

Beige (#f5f5dc):Used for the backgroung and section highlight to keep a fresh, natural feel.

Typography

Main Font: 'Poppins', san-serif | used for body text for a clean and modern look.

Accent Font: 'Merriweather', serif | used for headings and emhasized recipe titles to give a welcoming and trustworthy feel.
